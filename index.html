To participate you can sign up via the VBL SONA participation pool (or join the pool first) by clicking the links below.

What does participation involve?

  • Two short lab sessions at the Applied Behavioural Science Lab in the Transitorium Building at VU Amsterdam.
  • Five brief daily surveys on your phone during the 14-day study period. Each survey takes about 1 to 2 minutes.
  • A research app that logs when you use your phone. The app records app names and timestamps only. It does not collect message content or other personal content from your phone.
This study is designed to understand everyday smartphone use and attention. If the available study timeslots in the VBL SONA pool don't work for you, please contact the researcher if you are still interested in participating.

Frequently asked questions

Why is the study Android only?

The research app currently runs on Android devices, which is why participation is limited to Android users.

What phone data are collected?

The app logs app names and times of use. It does not collect message content, photos, typed text, or other personal content.

How much time does it take each day?

The daily surveys are brief. Across the day they take roughly 5 to 10 minutes in total.

How much can I earn?

You can earn up to €97.50 in total, including bonus payments linked to participation, with additional monetary prizes on top.
